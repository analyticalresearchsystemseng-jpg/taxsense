## Description

TaxSense gives UK workers a clear, real-time picture of their take-home pay — no guesswork, no surprises.

**Overtime Tracking**
Log overtime hours with multipliers (1.5x, 2.0x). See claimed and unclaimed overtime at a glance — claimed entries fold away so your outstanding overtime stands out. Tick the box once you've claimed it, and it rolls into the claimed section automatically. Export unclaimed overtime to CSV for expenses or payroll submissions.

**Real-Time Tax Calculations**
Enter your salary and tax code, and TaxSense calculates your income tax, National Insurance, and take-home pay instantly using current HMRC rates. Supports standard tax codes, Scottish rates, and emergency tax codes.

**Salary Sacrifice Efficiency**
See exactly how much you save with each salary sacrifice — pension, cycle-to-work, childcare, and more. A dedicated breakdown shows the efficiency of each sacrifice so you can make informed decisions.

**Self-Employed & Dual Income**
Switch between PAYE, self-employed, or both. Track self-employed income and expenses with annual profit projections. See how your employed and self-employed income combine for total tax liability.

**What-If Sandbox**
Test salary changes, pension adjustments, and overtime scenarios before they happen. See the impact on your take-home pay instantly without changing any real data.

**Mileage Tracking**
Log business miles and see if you're within the HMRC 10,000-mile threshold. Automatic AMAP rate calculations with projected annual mileage.

**Tax Traps**
Get alerts when you're approaching a tax trap — higher-rate thresholds, personal allowance tapering, or unexpected marginal rates. Never get caught off guard again.

**Full Annual Report**
Export a detailed annual breakdown including month-by-month income, tax, NI, pension, overtime, and deductions. CSV format ready for spreadsheets or submissions.

**Key Features:**
- Overtime tracker with claimed/unclaimed split
- UK tax calculator with current HMRC rates
- Salary sacrifice efficiency breakdown
- Self-employed income & expense tracking
- Dual income (PAYE + self-employed) support
- What-if sandbox for salary scenarios
- Mileage tracking with AMAP rates
- Tax trap warnings
- CSV export for payroll & expenses
- Dark & light mode
- All data stored locally on your device — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking

Built for UK workers who want to understand their pay, maximise their claims, and never miss overtime again.
