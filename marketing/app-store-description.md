## Description

**Your complete UK tax companion. Track PAYE, self-employment, and everything in between — all on your device.**

TaxSense is a privacy-first UK tax app that works entirely offline. Your financial data never leaves your phone: no accounts, no tracking, no ads.

**For Employees (PAYE):**
• Calculate take-home pay with real-time tax breakdowns
• Track overtime hours and earnings
• Monitor tax code changes and get variance alerts
• Model salary changes with the What-If Sandbox
• Add recurring bonuses, salary sacrifices, and deductions
• Get smart projections for tax set-aside and safe-to-spend amounts

**For Self-Employed:**
• Log income and expenses by month
• Track business mileage and compare mileage vs actual costs
• Record assets and calculate capital allowances (AIA)
• Estimate Self Assessment tax liability
• Use the £1,000 trading allowance or actual expenses

**For Everyone:**
✅ **Private** — Everything stays on your device, always
✅ **Offline** — Works without internet
✅ **Accurate** — Updated for 2026/27 HMRC rates
✅ **Simple** — Clean design, no clutter
✅ **Free** — No in-app purchases, ever

**Key Features:**
• What-If Sandbox — Experiment with salary changes without affecting your data
• Tax Code Alerts — Know when your code changes
• 60% Tax Trap Detection — Identify when you're losing your personal allowance
• Joint Mode — Manage PAYE + self-employment together
• Mileage Comparison — See whether simplified expenses or actual costs save you more
• Year-to-Year Copy — Transfer settings between tax years
