Frequently Asked Questions

Is TaxSense really free?

Yes. No in-app purchases, no subscription, no "pro" tier. Completely free.

Does TaxSense work offline?

Yes. All calculations happen on your device. No internet connection needed.

Is my financial data safe?

Your data stays on your phone — it's never uploaded or shared. TaxSense doesn't have servers.

Who is TaxSense for?

UK taxpayers who want to track their salary, overtime, self-employment income, expenses, mileage, and tax estimates — all in one place.

Can I use it for both PAYE and self-employment?

Yes. TaxSense has a Joint Mode that lets you manage both together.

Is this financial advice?

No. TaxSense provides calculations for guidance only. It is not a substitute for professional financial or tax advice. Always consult a qualified accountant or tax advisor for personalised guidance.

TaxSense is not affiliated with HMRC. Calculations are for guidance only. For official tax advice, visit gov.uk.
